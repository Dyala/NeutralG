Project Description

Carbon Action Plan
As part of the McDonald’s Asia Pacific Middle East and Africa sustainability strategy work, The Neutral Group designed a Carbon Action Plan (CAP) for HAVI Logistics’ distribution centre in Guangzhou, PRC. The TNG team carried out a detailed engineering site visit to install remote electricity monitoring for the site and to assess its carbon footprint, energy consumption and operational practises. The CAP then set out a number of equipment retrofit measures and business cases.
