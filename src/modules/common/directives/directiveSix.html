Service Description

Transport Operation Assessment and Fuel Reduction Programs

Service Details

Neutral Consulting specialises in the assessment of efficiency within transport operations and in the implementation of fuel reduction programs involving vehicle telematics, behaviour change and novel technology trials.

DX, the UK private mail company, requested a five year expansion strategy for its intelligent parcel drop box product Parcel Xchange in the B2B and B2C markets.

An in-depth market analysis was conducted in both Business to Business and Business to Consumer markets to create a target market and value proposition.

An optimal deployment network was developed together with a design and sourcing plan incorporated within a five year business plan.

    Benefits:

  • A strategic plan with informed priority options.
  • An executable implementation plan with a detailed budget.
  • A plan to optimise the current network and reduce the future unit cost by 20%.
