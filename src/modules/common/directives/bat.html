Project Description

warehousing managed by DHL - Energy monitoring and employee engagement

Provision of on-site electricity and gas consumption data with recording and analysis of temperature and humidity data in site warehousing led to a better strategy for controlling and operating site heating. This was implemented along with an employee engagement programme to manage energy usage better after new warehouse operations during a cold spell increased gas consumption hugely.

    Direct Benefits:

  • BAT Zevenaar is an old cigarette manufacturing site which now provides over 50,000 m2 of warehousing between 14 buildings
